Mademoiselle Caroline Riviére

by Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres

The portrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Riviére was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean Auguste Dominique
Ingres, and today hangs in the Louvre. It is the third of three portraits of the Riviére family the artist painted that year.

Date:1806
Medium:Oil on Canvass
Dimensions:100 x 700 cm
Home:Musée du Louvre, Paris
